Membrane ANM (membrANM)

membrANM is a suite of tools for investigating the global structural dynamics of transporters and other membrane proteins.

Features

membrANM can be used for

  • Constructing coarse-grained membrane models for use in ANM.
  • Comparing protein global motions in the presence and absence of membrane.

Download

membrANM tutorial files can be obtained from Getting membrANM.

Reference
Lezon TR, Bahar I Constraints Imposed by the Membrane Selectively Guide the Alternating Access Dynamics of the Glutamate Transporter GltPh 2012 Biophys J 102(6):1331-1340.
